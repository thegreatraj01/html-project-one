About Us:

Bihar Sharif is a historic city in the Nalanda district of Bihar, India. It was once the capital of the Pala dynasty, a Buddhist empire that ruled eastern India from the 8th to the12th centuries. Today, it is a pilgrimage site for both Buddhists and Muslims, who visit the ancient ruins of Nalanda University, the tomb of the Sufi saint Makhdoom Sharfuddin Ahmed Yahya Maneri, and the Bari Dargah, a complex of mosques and shrines. Bihar Sharif also offers a glimpse of the rural life and culture of Bihar, with its bustling markets, colorful festivals, and traditional cuisine.
